Integrity

Integrity is important to me because it makes things easier when you can be trusted to do the right thing even when nobody is around to keep you in check. I got this value from my parents, as they would teach me to act responsibly even when they aren't around.

Respect

Respect is important to me because I feel like everyone should treat others with respect, just as they would want other people to treat themselves respectfully. I also got this value from my parents, as it was imperative to treat them with respect if I wanted them to also treat me with respect.

Selflessness

Selflessness is important to me because I believe that the world doesn't revolve around you, and that in some instances, you could prioritize the betterment of others over yourself. I got this value from helping my friends with their mental health when I could have a lot on my own plate.
